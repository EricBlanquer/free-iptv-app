=== Note for Tester ===
No login required. To test the app:
1. Launch the app
2. Go to Settings
3. Add a playlist: select M3U type, enter URL: https://iptv-org.github.io/iptv/index.m3u
4. Save and return to Home
5. Select "Live TV" to browse channels
The app does not provide content - users supply their own M3U playlists or IPTV provider credentials.
