25TV_STANDARD1, 25TV_PREMIUM4/3/2/1, 25TV_BASIC3/2/1 (Tizen 9.0),

24TV_BASIC4, 24TV_BASIC2_LIC2/LIC (Tizen 8.0),
23TV_BASIC1_LIC2/LIC (Tizen 7.0)

=== Root Cause Analysis ===
The Note for Tester pointed to https://iptv-org.github.io/iptv/index.m3u — a raw aggregated playlist containing many geo-blocked and dead streams. From Samsung's test location (South Korea), the first channels clicked (e.g. TF1 HD marked [Geo-Blocked]) returned no playable variants, causing the tester to conclude the app couldn't play content.

=== Resolution ===
- Built dynamic geo-filtered playlist endpoint at https://iptv.blanquer.org/playlist.m3u (server-side country detection via ip-api.com, filtered iptv-org country/news/weather sources)
- Added welcome modal at first launch offering the demo playlist
- Auto-open Live TV when only live section is available
- Updated Note for Tester to instruct selecting "Yes" on the welcome popup
- Resubmitted as v1.0.5
