=== Submission ===
Version: 1.0.7
Date: 2026-05-02
Type: Resubmission after v1.0.6 rejection

=== Note for Tester ===
WHAT'S NEW

Fixes the v1.0.6 rejection ("Unable to play content"). v1.0.6 added a pre-flight internet probe to a single third-party endpoint (Cloudflare 1.1.1.1) before every playback. If that one endpoint was unreachable from the test network — even when the stream URLs themselves were reachable — the app refused to play and showed a "No internet connection" toast. v1.0.7 removes the third-party probe and uses the OS-level network state instead (Tizen webapis.network + navigator.onLine — instant, no HTTP call).

HOW TO TEST

No login or account required.

Option A — Try the bundled demo (recommended):
  1. On first launch, accept "Would you like to try a demo playlist?".
  2. App auto-jumps to Live TV with 7 channels (DW family, France 24 FR/EN, Al Jazeera English).
  3. Click any channel — should start playing within seconds.

Option B — Configure manually:
  Settings > Playlists > Add Playlist, enter an M3U URL, or Xtream credentials (server URL, username, password).
