After switching the playlist type to "M3U" in Settings > Playlists, the M3U URL input field could not receive focus (keyboard navigation couldn't reach it). The tester could not paste the M3U URL, effectively blocking any manual configuration path.

=== Resolution ===
Focus management fixed in v1.0.3: the M3U URL field now properly receives focus after switching playlist type to M3U. See v1.0.3/changelog.txt.
