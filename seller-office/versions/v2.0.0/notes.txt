=== Note for Tester ===
WHAT'S NEW

Major update (2.0). New "Aurora" home screen with artwork tiles and a refreshed look. Continue Watching, History and Favourites are now scoped to the active playlist. Fixed series resume opening season 1 instead of the last-watched season. Fixed movie descriptions briefly appearing then disappearing. Smoother playlist setup.

HOW TO TEST

No login or account required.

Option A - Bundled demo (recommended):
  1. On first launch, accept "Would you like to try a demo playlist?".
  2. The app jumps to Live TV with several channels (DW, France 24, Al Jazeera English).
  3. Click any channel - it should start playing within seconds.

Option B - Configure manually:
  Settings > Playlists > Add Playlist, then enter an M3U URL or Xtream credentials (server URL, username, password).
