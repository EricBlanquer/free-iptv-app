Free IPTV - Stream Your Content

Stream your personal IPTV content on your Samsung TV. This player supports M3U playlists, giving you full control over your media library.

Features:
- Live TV channels with EPG guide
- Movies (VOD) with poster artwork
- TV Series with season/episode navigation
- Multi-audio track support
- Subtitle support (embedded and external)
- Continue watching - resume where you left off
- Favorites and watch history
- Grid and list view modes
- Search and filter options
- 11 interface languages

The application does not provide any content. Users must configure their own IPTV playlists and are responsible for ensuring they have the legal right to access such content.

Requires: Internet connection, M3U playlist
