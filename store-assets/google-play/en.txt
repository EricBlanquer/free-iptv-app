Title: Free IPTV Player
Short description: Stream live TV, movies and series from your M3U playlists on any Android device.
Full description:
Free IPTV is a powerful media player for Android TV, phones and tablets. Stream your personal IPTV content with a modern, easy-to-use interface designed for both touch and remote control navigation.

FEATURES:
• Live TV channels with EPG (Electronic Program Guide)
• Movies (VOD) with poster artwork from TMDB
• TV Series with season and episode navigation
• Multi-audio track and subtitle support (embedded + external via OpenSubtitles / SubDL)
• Continue watching — resume playback where you left off
• Favorites, ratings and watch history
• Grid and list view modes
• Smart search and category filters
• Stream proxy support for VPN/routing setups
• Remote configuration from any web browser
• 11 interface languages: English, French, German, Spanish, Italian, Portuguese, Dutch, Polish, Russian, Arabic, Turkish
• Optimized for Android TV with D-pad navigation

SUPPORTED FORMATS:
• M3U / M3U8 playlists
• Xtream Codes API
• Jellyfin media servers
• HLS and DASH streaming (powered by ExoPlayer)

PRIVACY:
• No ads, no tracking, no data collection
• All settings stored locally on your device
• Open privacy policy: https://iptv.blanquer.org/privacy.html

DISCLAIMER:
This application does not provide any content. Users must configure their own IPTV playlists and are solely responsible for ensuring they have the legal right to access such content.

Requires: Internet connection, M3U playlist or Xtream Codes / Jellyfin server
